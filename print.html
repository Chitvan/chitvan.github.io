Making books

I've always been curious about Indesign. When I was in high school, I helped someone type their book for some pocket money. It was a maths book typed in a software I can't remember now, but working on it was not fun. So discovering the powers of Indesign was relieving!

I've thought about what to do with my art and books have always intrigued me. Specially children's books with art and story. For this project, I started with exploring layout ideas. I'd say typing the content was what took me the most time. And I've re-used it on this website, if you can't tell! Perhaps I can explore making vines in Indesign in future.

In making this website, I realized how I've just been using the same color combinations everywhere with shades of Blue and Teal. In my next project I'll probably try some thing new. No promises though!
